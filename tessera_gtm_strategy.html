The phase 1 rule: You are not running campaigns. You are having conversations. Every single client in month 1–3 should come from a direct, personal interaction. This is research disguised as sales — you're learning what objection kills the deal, what language makes people say yes, and what type of person converts fastest.

Your personal network — DM outreach
40–60 people you already know who fit the profile: tech professional, earns well, talks about health, had a health scare, watches a parent decline. Message them personally. Not a broadcast.
List 60 names — LinkedIn connections, college friends, ex-colleagues, gym buddies. Sort by: income signal + health interest + recent life event.
Offer a free "Biological Age Consultation" — 30 minutes, you walk them through what their biomarkers would likely reveal based on their lifestyle. No commitment needed.
Convert 20–30% of calls to paid clients at the Foundations tier. That's 12–18 calls → 3–5 clients from this channel alone.

Channel 04 · Long game
Doctor referral partnerships
Find 2–3 internists, cardiologists, or endocrinologists in Hyderabad who are seeing borderline patients: pre-diabetic, dyslipidemia, subclinical thyroid. Offer to be their lifestyle intervention arm.
The pitch to doctors: "Your patient needs lifestyle change but you don't have time to coach it. We do. You refer, we handle 90 days, we report back. You look better. ₹2,000 referral fee per enrolled client."

Proof — turn results into your growth engine
Month 4–9 · First case studies · B2B launch · Referral flywheel · Still minimal ad spend
50 cumulative clients
Phase 2 unlocks when: you have 3+ clients who completed Day 90, improved their biological age, and are willing to share their story. Before that, stay in Phase 1. Proof is what powers every channel in this phase.
Channel 1 — Case studies (your most powerful asset)
The perfect case study format
Before biological age: [X] years. Chronological age: [Y]. Gap: [Z] years of accelerated aging.
The 3 biggest findings: HOMA-IR of 3.2 (severe insulin resistance), Vitamin D of 18 (deficient), ApoB of 118 (cardiovascular risk).
What we did: Cut refined carbs, 150 min Zone 2/week, 3g EPA+DHA/day, fixed Vitamin D.
Day 90: Biological age dropped from 42 to 37. HOMA-IR halved. ApoB dropped 22%. HRV up 40%.
Client quote with their name and role. A photo if possible. Specific, not generic ("I feel amazing").
Publish: Full post on LinkedIn. Short version on Instagram. PDF on website. Reference in every sales conversation.
Channel 2 — HITEC City B2B corporate
The corporate outreach system
One corporate deal = ₹4–8L MRR. This is your fastest path from ₹50L to ₹2Cr ARR. Target: CHROs and founders of 50–300 person tech companies in HITEC City and Nanakramguda.
Target list: Identify 30 companies. Source CHROs via LinkedIn. Warm intro > cold outreach. Use your doctor advisor or investor network for intros.
The pitch frame: Not "wellness program." Frame as "Executive metabolic performance." Lead with: "Your senior leaders are your highest-value assets. We optimize them the same way your engineering team optimizes infrastructure — with data and measurement."
The pilot offer: 10 employees at 25% discount. After 90 days, they see the before/after biological age scores of 10 of their people. That outcome report sells the full program renewal.
Who to target first: Founders and CEOs of 50–150 person companies (faster decisions, CHRO = founder's spouse or direct report). Then mid-size IT services firms with established HR structure.

Channel 3 — Referral system (engineer it, don't hope for it)
The structured referral program
At Day 90, every client who improved their biological age is in the highest state of trust and enthusiasm they will ever be. This is your referral window. Engineer it, don't leave it to chance.
The Day 90 ask: "You've dropped 4.7 biological years in 90 days. Who in your network would actually do something with that information if you showed it to them? Can you introduce me to 2 people?"
Referral incentive: ₹2,000 account credit per referred client who enrolls. Or offer a free additional bloodwork panel (costs you ₹1,500, perceived value ₹5,000).
Make sharing easy: Give them a one-page PDF of their before/after results they can show a friend. Give them language: "I just finished a 90-day biological age program — my numbers actually moved. Here's who did it."
Channel 4 — Targeted LinkedIn content + early paid
Phase 2 LinkedIn shift: Move from "my personal journey" to "client outcomes + deep educational content." Post: breakdown of a real client's biomarker pattern (anonymized), what drove their protocol, what changed. This posts builds authority and generates inbound DMs at scale.
LinkedIn paid (₹15K–25K/month starting month 7)
Target: Hyderabad + Bangalore. Job titles: Software Engineer, Senior Engineer, Engineering Manager, Product Manager, Founder, VP, CXO. Age 28–48. Interests: health and wellness, fitness, Peter Attia, longevity.
Ad creative that works: Image of an actual biomarker result with the headline "This 36-year-old's body was aging 6 years faster than his age. Here's what changed in 90 days." Not stock photos. Not "transform your life."
CTA: "Get a free biological age consultation" → Calendly link. Do not send to a homepage. Direct to booking.
Expected metrics: ₹300–500 CPL (cost per lead), 20–25% lead-to-call conversion, 15–20% call-to-paid conversion. CAC ≈ ₹8,000–12,000 for paid channel.

Channel 1 — Content as distribution (YouTube + Instagram)
YouTube
Longevity Lab series
5–15 min educational videos. Not a vlog — a knowledge channel. Target: people Googling their biomarker values after getting bloodwork done.
SEO-targeted titles: "What does an HbA1c of 5.6 actually mean?" "Why your doctor says your LDL is fine but Peter Attia disagrees." "I tracked my biological age for 90 days."
Cadence: 1 video per week minimum. First 12 videos are your evergreen library. They compound over 12–18 months.
CTA: Free consultation booking link in description + pinned comment. YouTube leads convert at 12–18% to consultation because they've already watched 10 min of you.
Instagram
Biomarker education + client stories
Reels and carousels. Indian-context health content. "Why urban Indians get heart attacks at 45." "Your roti vs. millet — the insulin difference." Short, shareable, specific.
What works: Before/after biological age reveals (with client permission), myth-busting posts ("your corporate health checkup doesn't test for this"), infographic biomarker explainers.
Frequency: 4–5 posts/week. Reels: 3/week. Stories: daily. 80% education, 20% social proof and program.
Channel 2 — PR and media
Target publications and the exact pitch angle
YourStory / Inc42 / Mint Lounge: Angle: "Hyderabad startup is calculating biological age for urban professionals — here's what they're finding about Indian metabolic health." One feature = 20–40 warm inbound leads. Send your data: "Our clients' average HOMA-IR is X, average biological age gap is Y years." Real data, not PR fluff.
ET Health World / Hindustan Times Health: Angle: "Why urban Indian professionals are aging faster than their Western counterparts — and what longevity science says about it." Position as commentary, not self-promotion.
Podcast circuit: Target: Indian startup podcasts (Nikhil Kamath's shows, Raj Shamani, Ishan Sharma). Your story is genuinely interesting: "I built a system to slow aging using bloodwork and data." This gets booked.
Channel 3 — Community (your referral moat)
Private Longevity Circle — WhatsApp or Circle.so
A private community for current and alumni clients. This is your most powerful retention and referral engine. People stay because of the community, not just the program.
What happens inside: Weekly educational post from you. Members share their wearable data and ask questions. Monthly group call on a longevity topic. Peer accountability for protocols.
Why it drives referrals: Members show their metrics to friends. Friends see real people with real results. They DM you. Your community members become your salesforce.
Size target: 200 members by month 18. Each member refers on average 0.5 clients per year = 100 organic referrals/year at zero CAC.
Channel 4 — Bangalore expansion
When to expand to Bangalore: When you have ₹50L ARR, 50+ clients, and a waiting list in Hyderabad. Bangalore expansion is the same playbook — but you need a local health coach hire in Bangalore first. Do not try to manage Bangalore clients remotely from Hyderabad for the first 6 months.

MON 16–18
₹1Cr ARR — seed round preparation
Platform launched with biomarker dashboard. Begin seed round preparation: deck, financials, data room. 5 corporate accounts active. Community: 150+ members. YouTube: 50+ videos, 10K+ subscribers. First peer-reviewed dataset analysis prepared.
Target: ₹35–40L/month · ₹1Cr+ ARR confirmed
The honest timeline truth: Month 1–3 will feel slow. You'll be sending DMs and running workshops for ₹90K/month. That's fine — those clients and their case studies are the foundation that makes month 10–18 possible. The compounding is real, but it starts slow.
